चतुर्वर्ण्य आश्रम व्यवस्था

The Four Stages of Life in Sanatan Dharma

1. Brahmacharya (ब्रह्मचर्य)

Age: Birth – 25
The stage of student life, discipline, celibacy, and scriptural education. Focus on learning and character building.

“विद्या विनयेन शोभते” – Knowledge is adorned by humility.

2. Grihastha (गृहस्थ)

Age: 25 – 50
Householder stage involving marriage, family, work, and service to society. Duties include righteous earning and supporting others.

“धर्मेण अर्थं, धर्मेण कामं” – Earn and enjoy by following Dharma.

3. Vanaprastha (वानप्रस्थ)

Age: 50 – 75
Retirement stage of detachment, spiritual study, and gradual withdrawal from household duties.

“त्यागेनैके अमृतत्वमानशुः” – Only by renunciation does one attain immortality.

4. Sannyasa (संन्यास)

Age: 75+ or when ready
The stage of complete renunciation and spiritual liberation. Focus on moksha and inner realization.

“सर्वं त्यक्त्वा मम शरणं वृज” – Abandon all duties and take refuge in Me alone.
