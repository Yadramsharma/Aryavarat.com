🏹 What You’ll Find Inside

This sacred space is dedicated to three divine dimensions:



🔱 1. Sanatan Dharma – The Eternal Way of Life

Sanatan Dharma is not a religion; it is a way of being. It teaches us how to live in harmony with nature, self, society, and the divine. In this section, we explore the core principles of Hindu dharma—like karma, rebirth, dharma, moksha, the four ashrams of life, and the purusharthas. You will also learn about Indian festivals, rituals, sacred symbols, spiritual practices, and the deep philosophical ideas that shaped yoga, meditation, and holistic living. Whether you’re curious about the Upanayana sanskara or the meaning behind aarti, this section guides you into the heart of ancient wisdom.



🌺 2. Devi-Devta – Gods, Goddesses, and Divine Powers

Who is Shiva? What are the ten avatars of Vishnu? Why do we worship Ganesha before every task? How is Durga different from Kali? In the Devi-Devta section, we walk you through the divine family of Hindu gods and goddesses—each with unique symbolism, stories, temples, powers, and teachings. You will find detailed information about Trimurti (Brahma-Vishnu-Shiva), Tridevi (Saraswati-Lakshmi-Parvati), Hanuman, Kartikeya, Krishna, Rama, Radha, and many more. Whether you're a devotee or a learner, you’ll find spiritual joy in every paragraph.



🕉️ 3. Yug Gyaan – Timelines of Creation

How old is the universe according to Hindu scriptures? What happens in each Yuga? Where are we now in the cosmic cycle? This section is your spiritual time machine—explaining the vast timeline of Satya Yuga, Treta Yuga, Dwapar Yuga, and Kali Yuga. You’ll discover how Dharma reduces in each yuga, why avatars descend, what the signs of Kali Yuga are, and how Kalki will bring the next transition. This section offers a blend of scriptural reference, storytelling, and cosmic curiosity.
